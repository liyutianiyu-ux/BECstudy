听力专区 · Listening

听力技巧全攻略

40分钟 · 三大题型 · 30题

Part 1 — 12题
简短对话 Short Conversations
场景：3组商务简短对话，每组4道选择题
技巧：听关键数字、日期和名字，选项中有数字时特别注意改变，"not A but B"陷阱超常见
Common trap: "Actually, it's not Tuesday — we changed it to Thursday." → 答案是Thursday
Part 2 — 12题
段落笔记 / 填表 Note Completion
场景：2段商务独白（电话、报告、会议），每段6空
技巧：提前30秒读题目，预测词性（名词/数字/地点），听到signpost words如"however / due to / as a result"立刻警觉
填词规则：通常填1-3个词，拼写要正确，注意单复数
Part 3 — 6题
长段对话 Extended Discussion
场景：1段长对话（约5分钟），通常是两人商务讨论，6道三选一
技巧：听说话人的态度和观点，不是只听事实，注意"I think / I feel / I doubt"等表达观点的词
高分关键：听"转折"——最后说的意见往往才是答案
高频听力词汇 Signal Words
转折 Contrast
however · although · despite · on the other hand · even so · yet
原因 Cause
due to · as a result of · owing to · since · because · given that
结果 Result
therefore · consequently · as a result · this led to · hence
列举 Listing
firstly · in addition · furthermore · finally · also · not only

各题型攻略 Part-by-Part Tips
1
Part 1 — 选句填段 Sentence Completion
先读原文句子前后的语境，找logic clues（因果/转折/举例），选项中有代词如"this / their"时，看上文名词是否匹配。排除法很好用，先填确定的。
2
Part 2 — 段落标题匹配 Matching Headings
先读标题选项，找关键词；再快速skimming每段首尾句。注意：正确标题概括整段主旨，不是某个细节。干扰项往往出现在段落里，但不是主题。
3
Part 3 — 长文选择 Multiple Choice
先读问题，带着问题去定位原文段落。答案区域通常在一两句话内。注意paraphrase（同义改写）——原文说"reduce"，选项可能说"lower/cut"。
4
Part 4 — 词汇填空 Cloze
四选一，考搭配（collocation）比考意思更多。先看词性，再看上下文固定短语。高频考点：make/do/take的搭配，介词短语，形容词辨析（economic vs economical）。
5
Part 5 — 错误改正 Error Correction
每行只有0或1个多余词，没有多余词的行写"correct"。常见多余词：冠词（the/a多余）、介词多余、助动词多余。通读全段理解意思再逐行检查，不要孤立看每行。

口语题库 · Speaking

BEC口语三大Part

16分钟 · 2人同时考 · 商务情景讨论

约3分钟 · 考官提问
Tell me about your current job and main responsibilities.
关键：说清楚 job title / industry / key tasks，用商务词汇。避免说"I do many things"，要具体。
"I'm currently working as a UI/UX designer at a design agency in Singapore. My main responsibilities include leading visual design projects for our clients, collaborating with product managers, and ensuring brand consistency across platforms. I also oversee junior designers from time to time."
Part 1 常考题
What skills do you think are most important for success in business today?
结构：列举2-3点 + 各给一句理由。用"I would argue that / In my view / It's my belief that"引出观点。
"I would argue that communication skills are paramount, as the ability to convey ideas clearly can make or break a deal. In addition, adaptability has become increasingly important given the rapid changes in technology and market conditions."
Mini-Presentation结构（1分钟）： 引出话题 (10s) → 观点1+理由 (20s) → 观点2+理由 (20s) → 总结 (10s)
约1分钟 · 提前准备
Topic card: "The importance of customer feedback for a business"
卡片会给你一个商务话题，有1分钟准备，然后讲约1分钟，搭档可以提问。
"I'd like to talk about why customer feedback is vital for any business. Firstly, feedback helps companies identify weaknesses in their products or services before they become serious issues. A company that listens to customers can act proactively rather than reactively. Secondly, responding to feedback builds customer loyalty — people feel valued when their opinions are taken on board. In conclusion, feedback is not just useful data; it's a key tool for continuous improvement."

Part 3 高分秘诀：主动发言 + 礼貌回应搭档 + 引导讨论到结论。不要只说自己的观点，要互动。
约7分钟 · 双人讨论
Task: Your company wants to improve communication between departments. Discuss which methods would be most effective.
实用讨论表达 Useful Phrases
提出观点： "I think we should consider... / One option worth exploring is..."
礼貌同意： "That's a valid point. I completely agree that..." / "You're right, and I'd add that..."
礼貌反对： "I see your point, but I'm not entirely sure... / I take a slightly different view on this."
引导总结： "Shall we move on to...? / I think we've covered ___, what about...?"
下结论： "So, to summarise what we've agreed... / Overall, it seems the best approach would be..."

"Let's take that offline." — 私下讨论（不占用会议时间）

发邮件 Professional Emails
